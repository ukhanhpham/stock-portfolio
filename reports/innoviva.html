Investment Thesis

A diversified biopharmaceutical platform

Innoviva combines established respiratory royalty income, specialty therapeutics, infectious-disease products, strategic healthcare investments, and additional growth opportunities through the LYNX long-acting drug-delivery platform.

Thesis Drivers

  • Launch of Nortiva Bio and the LYNX long-acting oral drug-delivery platform.
  • International expansion of XACDURO through licensing and distribution partnerships.
  • A diversified portfolio of strategic healthcare assets.
  • Hospital reimbursement support and new infectious-disease product launches.

Key Risks

  • Commercial adoption risk for newer products.
